# Haibo! Privacy Policy

**Effective date**: 2026-04-16
**Last updated**: 2026-04-16

This policy explains what data Haibo! ("we", "us") collects about you,
why we collect it, and what rights you have over it. It applies to the
Haibo! mobile app (Android + iOS), our website at haibo.africa, and the
Haibo! Command Centre used by our moderators.

Haibo! is operated by **Haibo Africa** (Pty) Ltd — a company registered
in the Republic of South Africa. Our registered address is available on
request from hello@haibo.africa. We comply with the **Protection of Personal
Information Act (POPIA)** and, where applicable, the EU's GDPR.

## 1. What we collect

**Account data** — your name, email, phone number, profile picture, and
password hash. This is required to sign in.

**Location data** — we use your precise location (a) to show nearby taxi
ranks on the map, (b) to attach a pin to an SOS alert so your emergency
contacts know where to find you, and (c) to share your live trip if you
enable Trip Share. Location is only captured while the app is in the
foreground; we do not track you in the background.

**Payment data** — if you use the Wallet, your payment card is handled by
**Paystack**, a PCI-DSS compliant processor. We never see or store your
full card number. We store a tokenised reference plus your transaction
history for fraud prevention and dispute handling.

**User content** — photos, short videos (Phusha reels), voice notes on
SOS alerts or incident reports, community posts and comments, ratings,
and lost-and-found listings. This content is stored on our servers and
may be visible to other Haibo! users depending on the feature.

**Driver / vendor KYC data** (drivers and vendors only) — drivers upload
a selfie, an ID photo, and a driver's licence. Vendors upload business
registration and a bank confirmation letter. This is used solely to
verify identity and is not shared publicly.

**Emergency contact data** — the name and phone number of people you list
as your emergency contacts. This is entered manually; Haibo! does not
read your phone's contact list.

**Device data** — device model, OS version, app version, push
notification token, and crash reports. We use this to debug issues
and improve reliability.

**App activity** — which screens you visit, what you search for, and
which community posts you interact with. We use this to understand
which features work and to detect abuse.

## 2. What we DON'T collect

- Your phone contacts (we only store the ones you enter manually)
- Your SMS messages or call logs
- Your health, fitness, or fertility data
- Your web browsing history outside the app
- Advertising IDs (we don't show ads)
- Your race, religion, political affiliation, sexual orientation, or
  similar protected attributes

## 3. Who we share it with

We do **not** sell your data. We share it only with service providers we
need to run Haibo!:

- **Microsoft Azure** — hosting (in South Africa and North Europe regions)
- **Paystack** — payment processing (wallet top-ups, tips)
- **Expo / Google / Apple** — push notifications
- **BulkSMS** — one-time-password delivery
- **Sentry** (if enabled) — crash reporting
- **Law enforcement** — only if we receive a lawful order (court order,
  subpoena) or in a genuine, time-critical safety emergency

Your public profile and the community content you post (reels, posts,
ratings, reviews) is visible to other Haibo! users inside the app.

## 4. How long we keep it

- Account data: as long as your account exists, plus 30 days after
  deletion (for backup rollover).
- SOS alerts and emergency records: 7 years (in case of legal follow-up).
- Wallet transactions: 7 years (SARS / FICA requirement).
- KYC documents: 5 years after the account is closed, per FICA.
- Analytics and crash logs: 90 days, then aggregated.
- User-generated content (posts, reels): until you delete it, or until
  we remove it under our Community Guidelines.

## 5. Your rights under POPIA

You can at any time:

- **Access** — see what data we hold about you (email hello@haibo.africa).
- **Correct** — fix anything that's wrong (mostly editable in-app, email
  for anything else).
- **Delete** — close your account and erase your data. Tap Profile →
  Settings → Delete account, or email hello@haibo.africa. Some data may
  be retained for as long as the law requires (see section 4).
- **Object / Restrict** — tell us to stop a specific processing activity.
- **Complain** — to South Africa's Information Regulator at
  inforeg@justice.gov.za or www.justice.gov.za/inforeg/.

## 6. Children

Haibo! is intended for people aged 13 and over. If you believe a child
under 13 has created an account, email hello@haibo.africa and we will
remove it.

## 7. Security

We use TLS 1.2+ in transit and encrypted storage at rest. Passwords are
hashed with bcrypt. Access to production data is restricted to named
engineers under audit logging. If a breach affecting your data occurs,
we will notify you and the Information Regulator as required under POPIA.

## 8. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be announced in the app
and by push notification at least 14 days before they take effect.
